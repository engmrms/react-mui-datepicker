# Primitives Color

A primitive color is an individual color from our catalog of brand colors, gray.100, gary.200, etc. It is the most atomic form of color and will be attached to a semantic color name.

    <div className="flex flex-wrap gap-space-03">
        {value.map(e => (
            <ColorBlock key={e} color={e} value={e} />
        ))}
    </div>
</div>
))}
